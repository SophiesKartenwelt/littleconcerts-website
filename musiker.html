Good to Know

What makes us different

No pressure to be perfect

Babies cry, crawl, and babble. And that's completely fine. You play for an audience that is simply there. Not judging.

A small, warm gathering

Up to 8 families. It's a living room atmosphere, not a concert hall. You're close, it's personal.

Your repertoire

You choose your own programme. As long as it's classical. Violin, cello, flute, voice, or something else entirely.

A passion project

We don't take a cut. The entire fee from all attending families goes directly to you. That matters to us.
